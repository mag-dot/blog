## Prompt Structure Template**Follow this proven formula for pro results:​**

- Subject + Action: Clear main focus (e.g., "young woman screams in rage").

- Camera Move: Preset or description (e.g., "slow orbit with dolly-in").

- Lighting/Framing: Mood enhancers (e.g., "75mm lens, shallow depth, warm daylight").

- Style/Atmosphere: Film references (e.g., "filmic horror, high dynamic range").

Higgsfield Cinema Studio provides over 50 professional camera movement presets optimized for cinematic AI video generation from a single hero frame.
