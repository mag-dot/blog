# Elon Musk SpaceX xAI merger, Elon Musk net worth 2026, SpaceX xAI- Feb 4- 1 min readElon Musk announced a blockbuster merger between SpaceX and xAI on February 2, 2026, creating the world&#x27;s most valuable private company at $1.25 trillion. This SpaceX xAI merger integrates rockets, satellite internet, and AI to pioneer space-based data centers, potentially supercharging Musk&#x27;s empire ahead of a massive IPO.

## Elon Musk&#x27;s Mega Merger DetailsSpaceX, valued at around $1 trillion pre-merger, acquired xAI at a $250 billion valuation. SpaceX investors receive 80% of the combined entity, with xAI owners getting 20%, streamlining control under Musk who holds majority stakes in both. The deal, confirmed via Musk&#x27;s memo on SpaceX&#x27;s site, marks the largest merger ever and follows xAI&#x27;s prior integration with X (formerly Twitter). ([source](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-02-02/elon-musk-s-spacex-said-to-combine-with-xai-ahead-of-mega-ipo))

### Strategic RationaleMusk envisions orbital data centers to overcome earthly limits on AI power and cooling, calling space "the only path to scale" AI. This vertically integrates SpaceX&#x27;s Starlink broadband, rockets, and xAI&#x27;s Grok chatbot for real-time AI in space, amid Tesla&#x27;s recent $2 billion xAI investment. Critics question business logic, but it positions the entity for a summer IPO targeting over $1.5 trillion valuation.

### Elon Musk&#x27;s Net Worth ImpactPre-merger, Musk&#x27;s 42% SpaceX stake was worth ~$336-420 billion (at $800B-$1T valuation), plus 49% of xAI (~$122 billion at $250B). Post-merger, his ~43% in the $1.25T entity equates to $542 billion from this asset alone, pushing total net worth past $800 billion. This surge cements SpaceX as Musk&#x27;s top holding, outpacing Tesla, with IPO liquidity potentially adding billions more.
